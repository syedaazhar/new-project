About Sarah Haywood

Sarah Haywood planned her first party twenty years ago. What began as a sideline in party planning whilst working as a TV reporter and producer is now an award winning, international business.

The author of two wedding books, Sarah is consistently rated Britain’s top-selling bridal author. She regularly appears on radio and television in her capacity as a wedding expert and party planner; most notably as CNN's bridal expert for their worldwide simulcast on the Royal Wedding day of Prince William and Kate Middleton. She features in the lifestyle and bridal press both in the UK and overseas.

Sarah's Wedding Workshops for Bridal Professionals have been taken around the world to sell-out audiences. As a co-founder of EPIC, the Event Planners International Collaborative, Sarah jointly hosts residential Wedding Planning Courses.

An award winning business woman with a reputation for excellence, Sarah Haywood is in demand as a speaker at wedding and business conferences, and as a consultant for venues and hotels. As an expert in her field, Sarah has worked with a diverse array of international brands from Unilever to HBO.
